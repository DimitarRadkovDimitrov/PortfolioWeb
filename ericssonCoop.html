Ericsson Internship

About Ericsson

For my second, third, and fourth work-term I worked at Ericsson as a software developer intern focusing on the back-end of the stack.

Ericsson is a huge player in the global economy's information and communications technology (ICT) sector serving around 40% of the world's mobile traffic through their networks. To complement their telecommunications hardware Ericsson supports service providers with application development and maintenance for business operations and network management.

As a company they have around 100,000 employees worldwide with offices in Sweden, United States, and Canada just to name a few. I worked in Ericsson's Toronto office where the focus is on software research and development.
