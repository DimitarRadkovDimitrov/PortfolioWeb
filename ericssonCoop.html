About Ericsson

For my second, third, and fourth work-term I worked at Ericsson. Ericsson is a huge player in the global economy's information and communications technology (ICT) sector serving around 40% of the world's mobile traffic through their networks.

To complement their telecommunications hardware, Ericsson supports service providers with application development and maintenance for network and operations management and customer and revenue management.

As a company they have around 100,000 employees worldwide with offices in Toronto, Brazil, and India just to name a few. I worked at Ericsson's Toronto office where the focus is on research and development for software.
